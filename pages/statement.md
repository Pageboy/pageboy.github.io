### Fusing nature's colours with cohesive geometries

All works are wall hung; mostly panels of a 32mm depth.

![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53299807933_9663242d3d_h_d.jpg)

> **Rose Mallow** - UV varnished archival ink on fine art paper bonded to 2 panels 400mm x 565mm, bolted together - overall 565mm x 800mm

In my artistic journey, I have discovered a profound fascination with the harmonious interplay between the bountiful  natural world and the structured order of geometry. My digital collages represent a creative exploration which seamlessly blends the intricate details captured in nature photography with meticulously constructed geometric shapes that refract the hues of nature.

### My Creative Process

The creative process of blending photographs and geometry requires a careful balance between spontaneity and planning. In my practice, I explore various arrangements and geometric compositions to discover what generates the most resonant harmony. The unique flexibility afforded by digital tools offers scope for experiment, without the permanence of physical cut and paste methods, making it easier to revise, refine and reiterate.

![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53511325188_42a447f277_h_d.jpg)

> **Kandy Vanda** - UV varnished archival ink on fine art paper bonded to 3 panels , bolted together - overall 565mm x 917mm

### Visual Journey

My collages encapsulate the duality of creation - the vibrant spontaneity of nature and the calculated artistry of geometry. I arrange the geometric shapes, infusing them with purpose and intention. They guide the viewer’s eye, leading them on a visual journey through the composition. The marriage of photographic details and geometric shapes cultivates synergy that sparks curiosity and invites introspection.

![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53058778522_45dbf9474b_h_d.jpg)

> **Grass Splendour (Triptych)** - UV varnished archival ink on fine art paper bonded to 3 panels. Each one is 350mm x 565mm - overall 565mm x 1050mm. Panels are bolted together with stainless steel nuts and bolts.

### Digital Collage

In essence, my digital collages transcend the confines of traditional art forms. They embody a harmony that is both visual and philosophical, prompting viewers to contemplate the dialectical relationship between chaos and order, the ephemeral and the enduring. Through this creative process, I hope to foster a deeper connection between humanity and the natural world, encouraging us all to appreciate the beauty that surrounds us and the underlying unity that binds us together.
