Exotic colours are scarce in the winter garden, where the palette is subdued—mud, bark, and the fading greens of hardy foliage. But as spring begins to approach, the Lenten Rose (_Helleborus orientalis_) stands out beautifully against the damp soil. Its rich petals seem even more vivid after a night of rain.
