Along the riverbank beside the botanical gardens, the camera captures the vivid hues of a rare anemone. Its rich purples and soft mauves stand in striking contrast to the lush greens and warm yellows of the surrounding foliage.

I have partitioned the square into _golden rectangles_ and smaller squares, positioning the arcs so that each is centred on the opposite apex of the corresponding division.
