The Islands of Juan Fernandez

These images, are taken from engravings of drawings by Piercy Brett during his voyage on the Centurion with George Anson. These engravings, I presume, were taken from a copy of the 'authorised' account of the voyage, compiled by Richard Walter . These engravings are in my possesion. Captions are as they appear on the originals. Colouring is mine! Click over these images for VERY LARGE versions.
